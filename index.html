A high increase of pets put in shelters since the pandemic has been addressed.Pawsie's goal is to hopefully decrease that and find our pets its forever home. Pawsie is a non-profit organization teamed with the 11th Hour Rescue Center giving these animals a second chance at life.

Why Adopt?

  • You are saving a life.
  • Prevent the overpopulation
  • You are saving money
  • You get a trained pet
    • Great for mental health
  • Uncondtional love
  • Can benefit children
  • Encourages others to adopt

How to Adopt

  1. Go to the adopt section.
  2. Choose your new best friend.
  3. Read about your new best friend.
  4. Click on the "Adopt me" button.
  5. Fill out your information on the 11th Hour Rescue Shelters form.
